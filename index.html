Our Philosophy
"A room should feel like a portrait of the person who lives there — warm, considered, and entirely their own."

Founded in Paris in 1948 by cabinet-maker Henri Lacroix, MAISON has spent over seven decades proving that furniture can be both beautiful and immortal. Each piece begins as a sketch, evolves through rigorous craft, and arrives in your home as an heirloom.

Heritage Wood

Only sustainably sourced old-growth timber, seasoned for 24 months minimum.

Hand Joinery

Mortise and tenon, dovetail — no nails, no screws, just mastery.

Natural Finishes

Beeswax, linseed oil, and plant-based lacquers that age beautifully.

Bespoke Service

Every piece can be tailored to your dimensions, material, and finish.
